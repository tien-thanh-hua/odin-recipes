Instant Noodle

Description

Ingredients

  • 1-2 packs of instant noodle
  • 500ml of boiled water

Steps

  1. Start boiling the water.
  2. Tear open the cover(s).
  3. Put the noodle packs into the bowl.
  4. Add seasonings, noodle oil and dried vegetable (if available).
  5. (Optional) Add extra food such as eggs, bacon, etc.
  6. Once the water is boiled, pour it into the bowl.
  7. Cover the bowl for 3 minutes, and open it up.
