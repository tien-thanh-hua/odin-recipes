Fried Egg

Description

Ingredients

  • 1-2 chicken eggs
  • 50ml of oil

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan and add some oil.
  2. Crack open the egg.
  3. Pour the yolk and white onto the frying pan.
  4. Wait for the egg to gradually change color.
  5. Frequently change sides to avoid overfrying one side of an egg.
  6. Once both sides are done, take the fried egg out.
  7. (Optional) Add some pepper and spices, and you're good to go!
